Your data is recoverable only by you, using a unique key generated on your device.

The Architecture of Security

1. Client-Side Locking

Security starts on your device. Your files are locked securely before they ever leave your computer. This ensures that even our administrators cannot view your raw data.

2. Data Fragmentation

We split your locked data into unrecognizable "shards." These shards are distributed across independent storage nodes. A breach of one server yields only meaningless fragments, not your file.

3. Digital Camouflage

To prevent detection, your data fragments are embedded within ordinary digital assets. Your sensitive information effectively disappears into the digital noise of the internet.
