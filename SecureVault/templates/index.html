Encrypt Once.
Recover Reliably.

SecureVault protects your files using client-side AES encryption, ensuring your data is secured before it ever leaves your device.

Instead of storing encryption keys in one place, we split, conceal, and distribute them—so your data stays safe and recoverable even if parts of the system fail.

A Simple, Resilient Security Pipeline

1. Local Encryption

Files are encrypted on the user’s device using a unique AES key. The encrypted file can be safely uploaded or stored, while the plaintext data never leaves the device.

2. Key Splitting & Concealment

The encryption key is divided using a threshold scheme and each fragment is hidden inside ordinary images using steganography. Individual fragments reveal no useful information on their own.

3. Redundant Distribution & Recovery

Concealed key fragments are redundantly distributed across multiple cloud locations. Even if some images are deleted or become unavailable, the key can still be reconstructed securely.
